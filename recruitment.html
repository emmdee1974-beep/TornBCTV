Join the TornBC TV Network

We’re building a collaborative channel for Torn. If you’ve got a show idea, we want to hear it.

Who We’re Looking For

We’re especially interested in creators who bring something real from their game or real life: war reporters, faction leaders, traders, builders, storytellers, musicians, and quiet grinders with sharp minds.

Possible Show Types

  • Faction or war analysis segments
  • Travel diaries, hustles, or “day in the life” episodes
  • Guides for new players (earning, surviving, leveling)
  • Real-world DIY, repairs, and tools
  • Music blocks, playlists, or visualizers
  • Fiction, lore, or alternate-history storytelling

How to Apply

All applications go through in-game mail or forum message to LoneBlackBear. When you reach out, include:

  • Your in-game name
  • Working show title
  • Short description (2–4 sentences)
  • Format (live, recorded, voiceover, facecam, footage-only, etc.)
  • Rough episode length and how often you think you can post
  • Any links to previous videos or samples

Creator Expectations

  • Respectful to other players and cultures
  • Complies with Torn’s game rules and ToS
  • No hate, doxxing, or targeted harassment
  • We can discuss masking names/factions if needed

TornBC TV is a collaborative hobby project, not a company. For now, roles are volunteer and credit-based, but we’ll always be up front if anything changes.
