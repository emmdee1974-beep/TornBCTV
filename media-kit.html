Overview

What is TornBC TV?

Elevator Pitch
TornBC TV is an independent, fan-run broadcast channel for the browser game Torn. We produce news, DIY, money-market coverage, gossip, music, and original series for players who want something deeper than a single forum post or Discord ping.
Core Identity
A fictional TV station inside Torn, anchored by an on-screen persona – LoneBlackBear – who appears as a black bear news host. The style mixes classic TV news, light propaganda aesthetics, and grounded real-life builds and stories.
Primary Platforms
YouTube (full episodes and series) and TornBCTV.com (this site) as the hub for show pages, thumbnails, schedules, and recruitment.
Audience

Who Watches?

Demographics (Expected)
• Torn players and forum regulars
• Age range: largely 18–45
• Comfortable with long-term games, spreadsheets, and dark humour
Mindset
People who enjoy strategy, faction politics, side-hustles, and learning from others’ experiments in-game and in real life.
Reach & Metrics
As a growing project, we publish simple, honest stats rather than inflated promises. For current view counts and subscriber numbers, see the YouTube channel or contact LoneBlackBear for a latest snapshot.

Collaboration

Ways to Work With TornBC TV

Guest Segments & Interviews
Appear on TornBC Newsdesk or Talk & Tea to discuss wars, projects, guides, or community events.
Sponsored Segments (Game-Only)
For in-game organizations (factions, services, events), we can label short segments as “Presented by <Faction/Service>” with clear separation between editorial and sponsored content.
Cross-Promotions
Link trades, shared intros, joint events, or co-produced specials with other Torn YouTube channels and media projects.
Content Licensing
If you want to feature TornBC TV clips, news packages, or Thunder Maker footage in your own videos, you’re generally free to do so with visible credit to TornBC TV and LoneBlackBear. For anything more formal, contact us and we’ll figure it out.
Branding

Logo & Visual Style

Logo
The primary logo is a teal-and-silver “TornBC TV” wordmark often paired with a stylized “TB” badge. When in doubt, use the full wordmark on a dark background.
Colors
• Teal highlight: #2EC4C4
• Deep red accent: #D64045
• Dark teal background: #071216
• Neutral text: off-white and soft grey
Tone
Confident but grounded. Part serious newsroom, part scrappy underground station, with occasional tongue-in-cheek propaganda styling.
Bear Character
LoneBlackBear is depicted as a standing black bear in anchor attire (suit or tactical-black uniform), never as a joke mascot. He’s the anchor, not the punchline.
